Chai tea is an aromatic beverage that blends black tea with various herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 sugar
  • 1 1/2 unsweetened tea
  • 1 cup non fat dry milk
  • 1 cup French vanilla flavored powdered non-dairy creamer
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom

Steps

  1. Combine sugar, instant tea, milk powder, nondairy creamer, and vanilla-flavored creamer in a large bowl. Stir in ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom. Blend 1 cup at a time in a food processor or blender until a fine powder.
  2. Stir 2 heaping tablespoons chai tea mix into a mug of hot water for 1 serving.
